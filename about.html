About Us

Welcome to Parnix Beauty

Parnix Beauty Salon is the leading name in the beauty and cosmetology industry in Ghana. Geared to fashion-savvy clients, we are the largest, and most experienced growing salon in Ghana. Founded over 3 years now, we have grown in expertise and experience providing you with cutting edge professional hair care, nail care, foot care,massage and specialized body care solutions.

We consistently bring you the contemporary styles and ideas, by keeping up with the current trends and constantly educating ourselves about the best practices. Our professional stylists, therapists, barbers and beauty consultants are very well trained and highly experienced to give you an unforgettable experience. We represent your total beauty solution in Ghana and beyond, and hence, always strive to make your experience as unique and memorable as you are each and every time. We always adhere to excellent and exceptional customer service, as well as maintain the highest standard in the cleanliness of our environment with the view of exceeding your expectations, and keep you coming over and over!

CEO

Ms. Charlotte Wellington is the founder of Parnix beauty Saloon. At Parnix, we lay a lot of store on being an ethical, credible, reliable, trust-worthy, and quality-obsessed service provider with a customer-first approach which always puts the interests of our valued clients above that of our own. The Parnix Beauty Salon reflects these values in action.
